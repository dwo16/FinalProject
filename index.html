About the Artist

I created this website to showcase my mom’s watercolor artwork, which has been a huge part of my life growing up. She’s a lifelong artist who started in illustration and graphic design before returning to watercolor in 2012. Her paintings explore everyday moments, portraits, and the places that have shaped our family from our hometown river towns to the streets of New York and Philadelphia. Through this site, I just wanted to share her work, her stories, and the creativity that continues to inspire so many people.
